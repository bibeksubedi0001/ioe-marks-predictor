IOE Internal Marks Predictor

HOW TO USE
  1. Select your Faculty and Semester from the dropdowns above.
  2. Choose a Mode:
    • Predict — Enter your teacher-given assessment marks and external final marks to see your predicted grade sheet marks.
    • Reverse — Enter marks from your published grade sheet to reverse-calculate the teacher-given marks.
  3. Fill in the Obtained Marks columns. Results update instantly.
  4. Optionally enter your Name and Symbol No. for the header.
  5. Use Print or Save as Image to export your grade sheet.
